Display related tweets for your article from Twitter below the content of post of your blog. As seen on WordPress.com.
== Description ==
This plugin displays latest popular tweets from Twitter related to the tags in your post. You could associate the plugin with your authenticated account on inagist.com in which case it will diplay trending tweets from your account.

== Installation ==

1. Upload inagist-twitter-plugin.php and color.js to your /wp-content/plugins/ directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Do the configuration by clicking the Inagist Configuration link in plugins menu.
